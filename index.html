This page is here for a Rock Paper Scissors game in the Browser console.
Please open the browser console to play Rock Paper Scissors!

Please open the browser console and click here to start playing.

The rules:

  • Rock beats Scissors
  • Scissors beat Paper
  • Paper beats Rock.
  • If both players choose the same option, it is a tie.
  • In our game:
    • 5 Rounds!
    • For each round = 1 point to winner.
    • A tie will mean no one wins, and no points are attributed.
    • If user enters invalid entry round is counted as void - no points attributed.
